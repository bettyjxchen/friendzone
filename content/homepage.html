Hey there I'm Betty - a Chinese national with a Canadian citizenship hailing from Hong Kong and currently living in Los Angeles. In other words, I'm a 'third culture' kid who grew up in a melting pot throughout my life. Also why I speak four languages and love traveling. The adventure never stops.
On weekends, you'll either find me coffee shop hopping or building websites. There is an equally likely chance I'll just be eating something - not picky at all, I'll go from food trucks to drivethroughs to the fantastic DineLA restaurants in the city.

Full Stack Web Developer based in Los Angeles.

My journey with coding started with a budding interest during college. I've always considered myself a maker - the joy in building and creating fulfills me personally and professionally. I'm well-balanced in both my creative and logical sides, which is why I saw myself as a creative engineer.
I'm an experienced MEAN stack developer and love working in a team setting. My latest project is a web app built for Laso Concepts. My experience also includes an app built for Honda Innovations in the 2017 AutoMobility LA Hackathon. See my works.

Graduated UCLA in 2017 with a Bachelor's degree in Economics, and minors in Accounting and Digital Humanities.

I enjoy multi-disciplinary learning and enjoy practices which bridge the sciences to the arts, and the logic to the abstract. Especially with a background in Economics, I am shaped as a learner to identify trends and build theories upon logical patterns.
My comprehensive set of knowledge includes econometrics, data mapping, media analysis, and financial statements. My work experience in these fields include public accounting and marketing. Connect with me on LinkedIn.

Experienced in Branding and Marketing Technologies for Tech and Investment Companies.

I have a passion for helping companies and individuals build and establish a brand image in order to communicate the story behind a name. I am knowledgeable in driving business visibility to products while ensuring effective brand, product, and sales alignment.
My experience includes a winning media strategy for Snapple in the National Student Advertising Competition (NSAC) and former work experience at Belkin International, MBC Strategic, and Harper's Bazaar. Connect with me on LinkedIn.
