Snapple Campaign

National Student Advertising Competition 2016

1. Case Study

Developed a media strategy for Snapple to effectively reach the target demographic and ROI in both the heartland city of NY and non-heartland target cities.

2. Analysis

Conducted man on the street interviews, online surveys, focus groups along with feature association tests and brand perception maps to analyze consumer interest.

3. Execution

Analyzed consumer behavior data using Nielsen, eMarketer, and Kantar Media to provide actionable insight. Placed and costed executions, followed by evaluating post-campaign measures of effectiveness.
Project Details

Client: Dr. Pepper Snapple Group

Date: June 2016

Online: http://bit.ly/2D0cq3x

The Bruin Advertising and Marketing Team is an award-winning student-run organization that operates like a professional advertising agency. The team is composed of mini sub teams including media planning, strategic planning, creative, and design.
Each year, the Bruin AdTeam competes in NSAC, the National Student Advertising Competition hosted by the American Advertising Federation, in which teams across SoCal pitch a comprehensive media solution for clients which have included Snapple, Pizza Hut, and Nissan. This project placed second in 2016.
